JoRose Studios

Welcome to JoRose Studios! We are a small NFT studio with two artists and one developer/collector. What is an NFT Studio? Its a brand new space where artists, photographers, collectors and software developers get together and produce something new and inspiring and maybe bring a community together. We have many projects in full production right now and will be released late summer 2022 and and 10 percent of profits will go to Save the Rain Forest Foundation. Because the forest is our favorite place to hang out and we need to protect it. We are also collecting NFT art on most blockchains. We love NFT's and blockchain technology because it gives the power back to the little person and enables a free and open society.
